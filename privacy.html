Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Streamline AI Consulting collects, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We do not sell your personal information. Ever.

1. Information We Collect
Information you provide directly

When you fill out our intake form, contact us, or sign up for our services, we collect:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Business name(s) and industry
  • Details about your business operations you choose to share
  • Communications you send us via email or our contact form
Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may collect basic technical information including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website or source
  • General geographic location (city/region level, not precise)
Information from your business operations

As part of delivering our services, we may access or process business data you share with us — such as calendar entries, email content, invoices, or trip records — solely to build and operate your custom AI tools. This data is never used for any other purpose.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and deliver our services
  • Build, configure, and maintain your custom AI tools
  • Send you service-related communications (invoices, updates, support)
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to delivering services to you.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted tools we use to deliver services (e.g. Google Workspace, Anthropic API, Notion). These providers have their own privacy policies and are only given access to information necessary to perform their function.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfer: In the unlikely event of a sale or merger, your information may transfer as part of that transaction. We will notify you before that occurs.
4. Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with and rely on third-party platforms to function. These may include:

  • Google (Calendar, Gmail, Drive) — for briefing and scheduling tools
  • Anthropic — for AI processing and analysis
  • Notion — for task and project management integrations
  • Bambi — for NEMT trip management

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. By using our services, you acknowledge that data may be processed through these platforms. We only connect to services you explicitly authorize.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After termination of services, we retain basic account information for up to 12 months for billing and legal purposes, then securely delete it.

Business operational data (calendars, emails, trip records) accessed to power your tools is processed in real-time and is not stored permanently on our systems beyond what is required for the tool to function.

6. Security

We take security seriously. We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, access controls to limit who can view client data, and follow industry-standard practices for credential management.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at hello@streamlinedai.net.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@streamlinedai.net. We will respond within 30 days.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

As a California-based business serving California residents, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

To submit a CCPA request, email hello@streamlinedai.net with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and professionals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify active clients of material changes via email. The effective date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last updated. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
